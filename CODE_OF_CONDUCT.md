# Code of Conduct

ABC4RD Academy welcomes contributors from different countries, languages, disciplines, and backgrounds.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas and sources, not people.
- Keep discussions relevant to education, research, open source, Bitcoin, AI, and community work.
- Help newcomers understand the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private contact details or credentials.
- Spam or misleading partner claims.
- Unsupported claims presented as fact.
