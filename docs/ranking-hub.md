## Baseline Status

The `2026-05-12` direction tables remain the first-edition public baselines.

The `2026-05-15` cycle now adds the first dated current-cycle snapshot layer
and the first full published delta-note set.

That means:

- the scores are real;
- the source tables are real;
- the public publication is active;
- the current-cycle publication layer now exists;
- but no movement should be inferred unless a later pass publishes true
  rescored changes against this dated snapshot.

The next rescored Friday cycle will be the first movement-ready update.

## How Weekly Delta Will Work

From the next official weekly pass onward, each ranking will explain:

- who entered the Top 20;
- who left the Top 20;
- who moved up;
- who moved down;
- which official signal changed the score.

ABC4RD will only publish delta claims when two real dated source-backed weekly
snapshots exist.

## Public Response Rule

If an entity asks why it is ranked at a certain level, ABC4RD should respond
with:

1. the seven index scores;
2. the official links used;
3. the current dated source table;
4. the time window of the ranking cycle;
5. the specific index areas that limited the score.

This keeps the ranking explainable and reduces avoidable conflict.

## Safety Note

These rankings are public-signal assessments.

They do not claim:

- endorsement;
- partnership;
- investment quality;
- moral superiority;
- regulatory status beyond what official public sources clearly show.
