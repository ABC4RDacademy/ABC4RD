`Weekly Rank Score = TSI + OAI + EPI + KAI + CRI + TGI + MI`

- `TSI` = Technical Surface Index
- `OAI` = Open Activity Index
- `EPI` = Event Presence Index
- `KAI` = Knowledge Access Index
- `CRI` = Collaboration Readiness Index
- `TGI` = Trust And Governance Index

## Why The Leaders Are High

- The top group combines official docs, active GitHub surface, ecosystem
  programs, and clear protocol narratives.
- Ethereum, Solana, and Sui also showed strong current-cycle momentum through
  official 2026 protocol or platform updates.
- Bitcoin Core ranked unusually high on openness and governance legibility, but
  lower on event-facing visibility because its public surface is less conference
  centered than many foundation-led ecosystems.
