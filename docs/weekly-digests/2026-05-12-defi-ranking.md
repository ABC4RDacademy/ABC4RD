## Why The Leaders Are High

- Aave and Uniswap lead because they combine large-scale technical surface,
  deep developer readiness, public product execution, and strong governance
  visibility.
- Lido, Morpho, and Sky remain high because they connect staking, lending, and
  stablecoin yield into clear, institutionally legible operating stacks.
- Curve and Ethena score strongly because they continue shaping liquidity,
  stablecoin design, and DeFi credit infrastructure in visible public ways.

## Official Signals Reviewed This Cycle
