## Purpose

This is ABC4RD Academy's first cross-sector flagship ranking built from the
highest-scoring entities across all currently published blockchain direction
tables. It is designed to show which organizations combine technical depth,
open activity, event presence, knowledge access, collaboration readiness,
governance trust, and current momentum at the strongest overall level.

## Method

Formula:

`Weekly Rank Score = TSI + OAI + EPI + KAI + CRI + TGI + MI`

Tie-break rule for equal totals in this first cross-sector edition:

1. Higher `TGI`
2. Higher `MI`
3. Alphabetical order

## First Edition Note

This is the first cross-sector edition built on the expanded sector system. It
replaces the earlier pilot-style mixed watchlist as the main flagship ranking
for weekly publication.

## Why This List Matters

- It lets ABC4RD compare sectors without collapsing everything into a vague
  single-basket watchlist.
- It keeps the ranking explainable because every entry still resolves back to
  the same seven indices.
- It gives us a public flagship list that can be updated weekly with a clear
  delta note and source table.

## Official Signals Reviewed This Cycle

- Protocol leadership remains strong through the Ethereum Foundation and Solana
  Foundation public development and allocation signals.
- Payments and stablecoins remain exceptionally visible through Circle, Ripple,
  and Stripe / Bridge.
- DeFi continues to score highly through Aave and Uniswap.
- Institutional trust and interoperability remain strong through Chainalysis,
  Securitize, Chainlink, Digital Asset, and Fireblocks.
