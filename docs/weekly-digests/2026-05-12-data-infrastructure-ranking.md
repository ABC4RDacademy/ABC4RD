## Why The Leaders Are High

- Alchemy leads because it combines large-scale RPC operations, strong
  documentation, and very visible product momentum.
- The Graph, QuickNode, Chainlink, and Tenderly rank highly because they expose
  clear builder surfaces, active public roadmaps, and strong data-service
  positions.
- Etherscan, Helius, and Blockscout stay high because direct data access and
  public observability remain foundational to the sector.

## Official Signals Reviewed This Cycle

- Alchemy launched Solana gRPC on May 4, 2026 and followed with additional
  agent-wallet tooling on May 8, 2026.
- QuickNode published May 2026 platform updates and agentic infrastructure
  notes during the week of May 4 to May 6, 2026.
- The Graph published its 2026 technical roadmap describing multi-service data
  infrastructure for the onchain economy.
- Etherscan documented API policy and changelog updates on May 1, 2026.
- Tenderly, Helius, and Blockscout maintained visible public developer
  surfaces, which kept their knowledge-access and open-activity scores high.
