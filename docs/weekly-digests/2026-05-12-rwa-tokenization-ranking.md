## Why The Leaders Are High

- Securitize leads because it pairs institutional-grade tokenized fund scale
  with clear compliance positioning and repeated public distribution expansion.
- Ondo Finance and Centrifuge score highly because they go beyond issuance and
  actively connect tokenized assets to DeFi liquidity and market structure.
- Superstate, Archax, and Tokeny remain strong because they expose practical
  issuance workflows for funds, securities, and compliant secondary access.

## Official Signals Reviewed This Cycle

- Securitize continued expanding BUIDL and publicized fresh cross-chain and
  collateral integrations in early 2026.
- Ondo Finance launched its Global Markets Alliance to shape interoperability
  standards for tokenized securities.
- Centrifuge published its 2026 tokenization outlook and continued launching new
  tokenized products plus chain integrations.
- Superstate expanded FundOS and tokenized fund distribution in April 2026.
- Archax continued broadening regulated onchain RWA issuance in spring 2026.
