# ABC4RD Digital Health Lab Open-Source Stack Map

ABC4RD Digital Health Lab is the Academy block for FHIR, open electronic medical records, health data interoperability, synthetic patient data, clinical workflow literacy, and privacy-aware digital medicine education.

## First Scope

| Layer | Open-source stack | First pilot | Time | Cost |
|---|---|---|---:|---:|
| FHIR server | HAPI FHIR | synthetic patient FHIR sandbox | 1-3 days | $10-80/month |
| EMR education | OpenMRS, OpenEMR | demo patient workflow | 2-7 days | $20-150/month |
| Health analytics | OHDSI Atlas/WebAPI/CDM | cohort concept lab | 1-3 weeks | $50-300/month |
| Interoperability | OpenHIM | HIE routing concept | 3-14 days | $50-300/month |
| SMART apps | SMART on FHIR client | simple student app | 2-7 days | $0-100/month |
| Synthetic data | Synthea | fake patient dataset | same day-2 days | $0 |

## First Academy Product

Create a digital health sandbox with synthetic patients only: FHIR resources, patient timeline, consent notes, and interoperability diagrams.

Production note: no real patient data, no clinical claims, no diagnosis, and no medical device deployment without clinical, privacy, and legal review.
