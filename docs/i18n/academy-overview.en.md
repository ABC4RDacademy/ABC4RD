# ABC4RD Academy

Translation status: source.

ABC4RD Academy is an international research and education initiative for blockchain, Bitcoin, artificial intelligence, open compute, robotics, sensors, digital health, digital manufacturing, nanomaterials, networks, and verifiable digital infrastructure.

The Academy is being built as a public open-source educational ecosystem. It includes laboratories, curricula, source maps, digital publishing, media infrastructure, credentials, student portfolios, mentor pathways, challenges, and startup-style project pilots.

## What We Build

- Blockchain and Bitcoin education;
- AI production tools for websites, presentations, courses, certificates, videos, and research notes;
- digital library and digital press;
- media hub for lectures, streams, podcasts, and webinars;
- robotics, sensors, digital health, manufacturing, nanomaterials, compute, and network labs;
- Academy Operating System for learning paths, assignments, review, certificates, portfolios, mentors, careers, and venture pilots.

## Principle

ABC4RD Academy teaches technology through open sources, practical work, careful attribution, responsible experimentation, and public learning infrastructure.

## Disclaimer

ABC4RD Academy is an independent educational initiative. Unless explicitly stated, it is not officially affiliated with any referenced company, conference, university, sponsor, speaker, project, or organization. Public references are used for educational research, attribution, and community learning.
