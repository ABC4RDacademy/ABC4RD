## Month 2: Contributor Growth

- publish a good-first-issue index;
- publish one glossary issue per research track;
- publish one translation source-parity issue;
- create a public review checklist for course modules;
- publish a monthly progress report draft.

## Month 3: Public Proof

- close at least eight small public tasks;
- publish three weekly digests;
- publish one event or workshop outline;
- request source-backed feedback from one approved target;
- document every external response without private data.
