# Legal And Ethical Framework

ABC4RD Academy publishes educational materials only. This framework protects
students, contributors, and referenced communities.

## Financial And Crypto Disclaimer

ABC4RD Academy materials are educational and do not provide financial,
investment, trading, tax, or legal advice. Bitcoin, crypto, mining, and
token-related materials should focus on protocol literacy, open-source systems,
custody risks, and engineering tradeoffs.

## Medical Disclaimer

Digital health materials are educational and do not provide medical advice,
diagnosis, treatment guidance, clinical deployment instructions, or patient-care
recommendations. Do not use real patient data in ABC4RD Academy exercises.

## Robotics And Safety Disclaimer

Robotics materials are educational. Do not deploy student code in
safety-critical systems, industrial cells, vehicles, medical devices, or
physical robots without qualified supervision, risk assessment, and compliance
review.

## Nanomaterials Disclaimer

Nanomaterials materials are educational. Do not synthesize, handle, or test
nanomaterials without qualified laboratory supervision, safety procedures,
institutional approval, and applicable regulations.

## Licensing And Redistribution

- Link to third-party materials instead of copying them.
- Publish original summaries and exercises.
- Record license status.
- Do not upload pirated PDFs, copied standards, paid course content, or unclear
  mirrors.

## Contributor Privacy

- Do not collect private contact data in public issues.
- Do not publish private email, phone, CRM, contract, or medical data.
- Contributors may use public GitHub profiles only.
- Outreach drafts must be reviewable before sending.
