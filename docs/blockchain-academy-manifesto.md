# Blockchain Academy Manifesto

ABC4RD Academy teaches blockchain not as speculation, but as a discipline of
verifiable digital infrastructure.

Blockchain education should help students understand how people create,
verify, govern, and challenge digital claims. The point is not to put every
database on-chain. The point is to ask better questions about trust.

## Position

ABC4RD Academy is a Blockchain Academy for verifiable digital infrastructure.

That means blockchain is taught together with:

- Bitcoin and open monetary networks;
- Ethereum, smart contracts, and programmable settlement;
- RWA, tokenization, and digital assets;
- DAO and digital governance;
- AI, open compute, and verifiable computation;
- digital identity and credentials;
- applied infrastructure in health, manufacturing, robotics, sensors, and
  nanomaterials.

## Principles

- Proof before hype.
- Source before claim.
- Open source before marketing.
- Education before outreach.
- Verification before tokenization.
- Ethics before deployment.
- Narrow claims before broad narratives.
- Public artifacts before partnership requests.

## What We Teach

We teach students to ask:

- What is the claim?
- What is being verified?
- Who controls the data?
- What is on-chain and what is off-chain?
- Who controls the oracle?
- What can fail?
- What does the record not prove?
- What legal, safety, medical, financial, or IP boundary applies?

## What We Reject

ABC4RD Academy rejects:

- guaranteed-profit narratives;
- fake partnerships;
- unsupported tokenization claims;
- "blockchain solves everything" language;
- copying third-party materials without attribution;
- medical, safety, or legal claims without qualified review;
- mass outreach before public value exists.

## Public Promise

ABC4RD Academy will build public curricula, source maps, issues, discussions,
glossaries, and contribution paths that make blockchain education useful,
reviewable, and responsible.
