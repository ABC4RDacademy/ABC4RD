# Blockchain Research Index

This index organizes public blockchain research directions for ABC4RD Academy.

Each entry should include:

- source link;
- annotation;
- language;
- difficulty;
- license/reuse status;
- verification status;
- curriculum relevance.

## Sections

| Area | Questions |
|---|---|
| Bitcoin research | What does Bitcoin verify, and what remains social/off-chain? |
| Ethereum research | What does programmable settlement enable and risk? |
| Cryptography | What primitives make verification possible? |
| Consensus | How do networks agree under adversarial conditions? |
| Scaling | What moves to L2 and what trust assumptions change? |
| Privacy / ZK | What can be proven without revealing raw data? |
| MEV | Who can reorder, extract, or censor value? |
| Tokenization / RWA | What is the relationship between token and asset claim? |
| DAO governance | How do communities govern, execute, and fail? |
| Digital identity | What is identity, credential, key, and authorization? |
| Supply chains | What does traceability prove or fail to prove? |
| Health data | How do standards, privacy, and consent interact? |
| AI agents | How can actions, logs, and compute records be verified? |

## Review Rule

Do not add papers or reports from unclear reposts. Use official publisher,
university, standards body, project, or author links whenever possible.
