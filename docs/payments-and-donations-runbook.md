# ABC4RD Payments and Donations Runbook

Status: v0.1 draft.

This runbook explains how ABC4RD Academy can start accepting money responsibly.

## Step 1: Decide Legal Path

Choose one:

1. local legal entity;
2. US company route if appropriate;
3. fiscal host / Open Collective;
4. temporary no-revenue mode with waitlist only.

Do not launch paid checkout until the receiving entity/account is clear.

## Step 2: Open Business Account

Recommended rule:

Do not use personal accounts for long-term Academy money.

Needed:

- legal entity name;
- registration documents;
- beneficial owner identity;
- tax information;
- business address;
- website or GitHub/project description;
- expected activity description.

Possible account types:

- traditional bank business account;
- Wise Business where available;
- bank account connected to Stripe/PayPal;
- fiscal host account via Open Collective.

## Step 3: Pick Payment Channels

### Minimum setup

- bank transfer/invoice for sponsors and corporate clients;
- card payment provider for courses;
- donation platform for supporters;
- crypto donations only after crypto policy is approved.

### Suggested launch order

1. Donation waitlist page.
2. Invoice/bank transfer for sponsors.
3. Card checkout for paid cohort.
4. GitHub Sponsors or Open Collective.
5. BTCPay Server for Bitcoin donations.

## Step 4: Separate Money Types

Track separately:

- tuition/course revenue;
- certificate review fees;
- donations;
- grants;
- sponsorship;
- consulting/project revenue;
- crypto donations.

## Step 5: Create Public Policies

Required before launch:

- pricing policy;
- refund policy;
- donation policy;
- crypto donation policy;
- sponsor policy;
- privacy policy;
- no investment advice disclaimer;
- no accredited degree disclaimer.

## Step 6: Accounting Fields

For every payment:

- date;
- payer/donor name if available;
- amount;
- currency;
- payment method;
- purpose;
- invoice/receipt ID;
- refund status;
- tax status;
- notes;
- source campaign.

## Step 7: Donation Receipts

Unless ABC4RD is legally registered as a tax-deductible nonprofit in the donor's jurisdiction, do not tell donors their donation is tax-deductible.

Receipt wording:

`Thank you for supporting ABC4RD Academy. This receipt confirms payment received. It does not represent tax advice or confirmation of tax-deductible charitable status.`

## Step 8: Refund Rules

Draft refund policy:

- free materials: no payment needed;
- paid cohort: full refund before cohort starts;
- partial refund during first 20% of cohort;
- no refund after certificate review is completed;
- exceptions for duplicate payment or technical failure;
- crypto refunds reviewed manually due volatility and fees.

## Step 9: Crypto Handling

If crypto is accepted:

- start with Bitcoin/Lightning only;
- use BTCPay Server or reputable provider;
- record fiat value at receipt time;
- keep wallet backups offline;
- require multi-person approval for large transfers;
- publish wallet addresses only through verified Academy channels;
- screen large/suspicious donations;
- do not accept tokens tied to promotion.

## Step 10: First Launch Checklist

- [ ] Legal/account path selected.
- [ ] Business/fiscal account ready.
- [ ] Payment processor approved.
- [ ] Donation policy published.
- [ ] Refund policy published.
- [ ] Pricing page published.
- [ ] Invoice template ready.
- [ ] Crypto policy approved or crypto disabled.
- [ ] Test payment completed.
- [ ] Receipt email tested.
- [ ] Accounting sheet/database ready.
- [ ] Public disclaimer visible.
