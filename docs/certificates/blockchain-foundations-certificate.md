educational record, not an accredited degree, license, employment guarantee, or
financial qualification.

## Completion requirements

- Bitcoin foundations explanation.
- Transaction or state-transition diagram.
- ERC-20 localnet deployment log.
- Short reflection on blockchain trust limits.
- Public learning artifact reviewed by a maintainer.

## Review rules

- Use public GitHub evidence where possible.
- Do not publish private identity documents.
- Do not publish wallet seed phrases, private keys, or financial data.
- Do not issue certificates automatically until a maintainer approves the
  evidence record.
- Mark all early certificates as test certificates unless a formal credential
  policy is approved.
