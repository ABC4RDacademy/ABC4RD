# AI Voice Open-Source Watchlist

ABC4RD Academy is watching open-source AI voice repositories for multilingual
TTS, voice cloning, voice conversion, speech recognition, realtime voice agents,
dubbing, and speech research.

This is not a partnership list. ABC4RD Academy will not clone real voices,
imitate people, or use ethnic/national accents without clear consent and a safe
educational purpose.

## ABC4RD Academy Voice Rules

- No cloning real people without consent.
- No impersonation.
- No hidden synthetic voice use.
- No ethnic, national, or identity-based caricature.
- Use language, accessibility, education, and tone as primary routing factors.
- Keep accent/persona choices user-consented and respectful.
