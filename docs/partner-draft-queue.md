# Partner Draft Queue

All partner messages are drafts until a maintainer approves the target, wording, sender account, and timing.

## Draft lanes

| Lane | Purpose | First artifact |
| --- | --- | --- |
| GitLab Education | Explore education/open-source fit | program inquiry draft |
| Universities | Course and research collaboration | academic review draft |
| Bitcoin education | Workshops and learning artifacts | event-linked note |
| Open-source AI | Course labs and contributor pathways | module collaboration note |
| Science and ecology | Evidence, climate, compute, planetary health | science literacy note |

## Required fields

Every partner draft must include:

- target person, group, company, or event;
- public source URL;
- why ABC4RD should engage;
- draft message;
- expected useful artifact;
- follow-up owner;
- approval status.

## Boundary

Do not claim partnership, sponsorship, endorsement, or approval unless it is formally confirmed. Do not send messages from GitLab, email, Telegram, Discord, or X before approval.
