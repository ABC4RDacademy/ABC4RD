## Practical Rule

ABC4RD can teach blockchain and accept a reviewed crypto payment pilot, but it
should not behave like an exchange, broker, custodian, token issuer, investment
seller, or financial adviser.

## Review Questions

- Which operating entity receives fiat?
- Which jurisdictions are open for paid cohorts?
- Which payment processor is approved?
- Is crypto accepted at all during the first paid cohort?
- If crypto is accepted, which asset and wallet are approved?
- Is crypto converted to fiat immediately or held under a written policy?
- Who reviews refunds, sanctions risk, tax records, and accounting entries?
