# Money Flow v1

Status: operating draft. Not legal, tax, accounting, or financial advice.

This page defines where ABC4RD Academy receives value and how that value moves
through the operating model.

## Intake Points

| Intake point | Default use | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bank transfer | institutional invoices | recommended first |
| Card payment | individual cohorts and review fees | recommended first |
| Invoice sponsor payment | scholarships and sponsored cohorts | recommended first |
| Crypto payment wallet | limited education payment pilot | review required |
| Grant agreement | research, scholarships, open-source work | review required |
| In-kind contribution record | credits, tools, venues, equipment | review required |

## Default Flow

1. Buyer chooses an approved offer.
2. ABC4RD issues invoice, receipt, or sponsorship agreement.
3. Payment or contribution is recorded.
4. Refund and compliance status are recorded.
5. Value is assigned to a treasury bucket.
6. Delivery evidence is linked to the payment record.
7. Monthly review checks revenue, refunds, scholarships, and open obligations.

## Fiat Flow

Fiat payments should move into an operating account or payment processor account
owned by the approved ABC4RD operating entity.

Required record:

- invoice ID;
- payer;
- offer;
- currency;
- amount;
- date;
- payment method;
- refund status;
- tax/accounting category;
- delivery status.

## Crypto Flow

Crypto is a pilot path only.

Crypto payments should move into a labeled receiving wallet controlled by the
approved ABC4RD operating entity. Do not use a personal wallet for Academy
payments.

Required record:

- invoice ID;
- payer identity where required;
- asset;
- network;
- amount;
- fiat value at receipt time;
- transaction hash;
- receiving wallet;
- compliance status;
- conversion or hold decision;
- refund status.

## Non-Cash Flow

Grants, sponsorships, in-kind value, service exchange, and venture-style value
need written scope before acceptance.

Minimum record:

- contributor;
- value type;
- fair value if available;
- restrictions;
- delivery obligation;
- public naming rule;
- expiration or reporting deadline.

## Prohibited Flow

Do not receive money for:

- token appreciation;
- Academy token sales;
- trading advice;
- custody or managed wallets;
- guaranteed credential approval;
- official partner claims without written proof;
- services that require professional licensing unless reviewed.
