## Purpose

Turn the Launch Sprint 01 baseline into a repeatable Academy operating system.
Sprint 02 focuses on disciplined weekly movement instead of adding more tracks:

- Monday course update;
- Wednesday lab/demo update;
- Friday research bulletin;
- monthly certificate review batch;
- six-language entry review workflow.
