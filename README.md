# ABC4RD Academy

ABC4RD Academy is an international blockchain, AI, and science education initiative. We build open learning tracks, source-backed research notes, practical labs, and community pathways for students, researchers, developers, and partners.

## Start here

| You are | Go here | Useful output |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Scientist | [Science Labs](https://gitlab.com/abc4rd-academy/science-labs) | fundamental science notes, ecology, climate, compute |

## Flagship

LLMAIX2001a: Storyteller AI Course is the first flagship learning track. It moves from language-model foundations to transformers, deployment, multimodal systems, open-source contribution, AI + Bitcoin education, and science-linked learning artifacts.

## Operating principles

- Publish useful work every week: issues, merge requests, source notes, event watches, research briefs, and beginner tasks.
- Keep public claims source-backed.
- Treat outreach as draft-first until a maintainer approves it.
- Make contribution paths concrete: good first issues, module tasks, translation tasks, verification tasks, and lab tasks.
- Use English as the primary public language, with RU, ES, FR, ZH, and AR translation tracks.

## Ecosystem map

- ABC4RD: main hub, roadmap, publishing policy, and coordination.
- LLMAIX2001a: Storyteller AI course and AI education flagship.
- abc4rd-research: evidence, citations, public source review, and media-kit discipline.
- abc4rd-community: governance, onboarding, events, and public collaboration.
- bitcoin-education: Bitcoin foundations, Lightning, Nostr, custody, mining, and treasury literacy.
- science-labs: fundamental science, ecology, climate, compute, and research literacy.
- applied-research-labs: prototypes, reproducible demos, and practical open-source research.

## Growth metrics

We track contributors, merge requests, opened and closed issues, course modules, citations, translations, stars, forks, community members, and published learning artifacts.

## Safety

Do not commit secrets, private emails, internal contracts, or unapproved partner claims. Tokens belong only in local ignored files or GitLab CI/CD variables.
