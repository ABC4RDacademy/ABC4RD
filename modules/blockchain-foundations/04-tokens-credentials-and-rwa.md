Course: Blockchain Foundations: From Bitcoin to Verifiable Infrastructure.

Status: v0.1 draft.

## Goal

Understand the difference between a token, credential, NFT, legal claim, attestation, custody record, and real-world asset.

## Read

- W3C Verifiable Credentials overview;
- 1EdTech Open Badges overview;
- Ethereum ERCs/EIPs related to token standards;
- one public source explaining RWA tokenization risks.

## Learn

A token can represent a record, permission, claim, membership, credential, or asset reference. But a token does not automatically create legal ownership. The legal system, issuer obligations, custody, jurisdiction, and documentation matter.
