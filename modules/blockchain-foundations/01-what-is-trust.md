# Module 01: What Is Trust?

Course: Blockchain Foundations: From Bitcoin to Verifiable Infrastructure.

Status: v0.1 draft.

## Goal

Understand why people use ledgers, signatures, witnesses, institutions, audits, cryptography, and blockchains to coordinate trust.

## Read

- ABC4RD No Hype Policy;

- one public article or documentation page explaining digital signatures;
- one public source explaining why databases and blockchains solve different problems.

## Learn

A blockchain is not magic trust. It is a system for making some records difficult to alter without detection. It can verify rules inside the system, but it does not automatically prove that off-chain claims are true.
