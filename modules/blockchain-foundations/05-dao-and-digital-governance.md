# Module 05: DAO and Digital Governance

Course: Blockchain Foundations: From Bitcoin to Verifiable Infrastructure.

Status: v0.1 draft.

Time: 60-120 minutes.

## Goal

Understand DAO governance, proposals, voting, multisig treasury, delegation, roles, grants, and governance risk.

## Key Ideas

- DAO;
- proposal;
- voting;
- quorum;
- delegation;
- multisig;
- treasury;
- contributor role;
- grant;
- governance attack.

## Read

- Safe documentation;
- Snapshot documentation;
- Aragon documentation;
- OpenZeppelin Governor documentation or related source.

## Learn

A DAO is not just a token vote. It is a governance system with people, rules, treasury control, decision processes, accountability, and legal questions.

## Assignment

Draft a simple DAO proposal:

- title;
- purpose;
- requested budget;
- responsible person/team;
- voting rule;
- success metric;
- risk;
- fallback plan.

## Submission

Add your proposal to:

```text
student-submissions/blockchain-foundations/[github-username].md
```

## Review Criteria

- proposal has a clear purpose;
- budget and responsibility are visible;
- decision rule is stated;
- risk is not ignored;
- no fake legal DAO claim is made.

## Safety Note

Do not move treasury, governance rights, or contributor payments into production tools without legal, accounting, and security review.
