# Contributing

Thanks for helping build ABC4RD Academy in public.

## Good contributions

- Improve public wording.
- Add source-backed evidence.
- Propose a course module.
- Draft a workshop or event activation.
- Add a partner lead with a public source.
- Report broken links or unclear claims.

## Standards

- Keep pull requests small and reviewable.
- Include sources for external claims.
- Label uncertain items as drafts or proposals.
- Do not include secrets or private contact details.
- Use international positioning. Russia is historical 2017 context only.

## Review checklist

- The change helps learners, contributors, partners, or maintainers.
- Claims are backed by public sources.
- The tone is useful, not hype-driven.
- No credentials, private emails, or internal-only details are included.
