Production readiness gates

1. PurposeWhat problem is solved and why blockchain is needed.
2. Legal reviewToken rights, jurisdiction, financial and RWA risks.
3. Local testsFoundry/Hardhat, coverage, fuzzing, access checks.
4. TestnetPublic demo only after local tests and documentation.
5. AuditRequired before funds, credentials, assets, or governance power.
6. LaunchRunbook, monitoring, incident response, disclaimers.

Guardrail

All first ABC4RD blockchain pilots must be localnet or testnet. No investment promises, no public sale, no mainnet deployment, and no legal ownership claim without legal and security review.
